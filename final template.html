About as

        The college is established and administrated by the Noida & Greater Noida Education Society. The college is funded by

        Geography Lab, Junior Science Lab, Senior Science Lab, Mathematics Lab, English Lab, Vedic Modern Lab, I.C.T base
        Computer Lab, Music Room & Sports Room etc. Hostel facility is available only for the students of Classes VI to XII. The
        school BUS is available for class 1st to 5th.. We are imparting education in three streams: Science (PCM, PCB), Commerce
        and Humanities. The college aims at providing a value based and wholesome quality education to provide its students
        useful and worthy citizens of our country.
        
        We are having competent and capable teaching staff to educate the students by an integral education system. The
        efficient teachers aim at providing intellectual education through e-learning in each class room, social education by
        making the students realize that he is a member of society and through co-curricular activities, physical education is
        provided with the help of highly qualified instructors. We are having ample space for outdoor and indoor games.
        We offer a variety of clubs, extra classes supervised study and a wide range of activities to students at all levels.
        Special coaching is being organized for various activities. So that students also learn the lessons of team spirit,
        sharing, hope and participation. The college is a Residential college with mandatory hostel accommodation from class 6th
